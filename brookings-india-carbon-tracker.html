FAQs

Is it a live electricity and carbon tracker? Yes, it is a live tracker updated every 5 minutes.

Is the CO2 based on electricity generation or all CO2 generation including transportation etc? The CO2 is based on electricity generation connected to the grid.

Does it include the CO2 generation from off-grid sources like Diesel Gensets? No, the tracker is focused of CO2 generated from grid connected generation sources.

I just want to look at how a single parameter varies over time. How do I do that? You can do that by deselecting the other parameters and only selecting the one that you need.
